ABOUT US

BrewHaus is a student-run café located on Cabaguio Avenue, dedicated to celebrating Filipino coffee and culture. Our mission is to support local farmers, share the rich stories behind our coffee heritage, and reinvest our profits into educational initiatives. At BrewHaus, we offer a wide selection of coffee, tea, and pastries, all served in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. Beyond our physical space, we are committed to creating an engaging digital experience through a user-centered website designed with accessibility, research, and community feedback in mind. Through every cup and every story, BrewHaus strives to build a more sustainable, culturally enriching social hub for all.
